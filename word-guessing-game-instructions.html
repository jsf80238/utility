Word Guessing Game Instructions

Game.

A letter may appear in the answer more than once. The answer will never be a plural noun, for example ROCKS or HANDS will never be answers. The following colors indicate how a letter matches (or doesn't) the answer:

Letter does not exist in the word.
Letter exists in the word, but not here.
Letter is correctly placed.
